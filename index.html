Fictional place for remains

The starting point of this project is Swansong.
Reading this article describing the situation of World War II, we imagined the atmosphere of war, and made it the center of the project.
We wanted to recreate the atmosphere of war (or post-war) through this project.
So we had to add a virtual imagination to it based on things we could access or collect.
The chosen materials are urban spaces with traces of war left.
We decided to collect the traces of the war left in the modern city and recombine it to build a virtual space (city) on the Web to recreate the atmosphere of the war felt in the Swan Song text.
